###[ When Should You Hire](https://allseasongutter.com/blog/best-time-of-year-to-clean-gutters/) a Gutter Cleaning Service?

Clogged gutters are more than just an eyesore—they can cause long-term damage if left unchecked. Here are a few signs it might be time to call in the pros:

**Water is spilling over the edges** of your gutters during rainstorms. That “waterfall” effect means they’re clogged and not draining properly.

**Your gutters are sagging or pulling away** from your home. Excess weight from standing water and debris can strain the brackets.

**Stains or mildew are forming** on your siding. This often happens when water overflows and runs down the exterior walls.

**Plants are sprouting** in the gutters. If greenery is growing, it means the debris has been sitting for quite some time.

**Leaks are showing up** on your walls or ceiling. Overflowing gutters can push water into areas it shouldn’t be—inside your home.

**Water is seeping into your basement.** Improper drainage near your foundation is a recipe for moisture problems below ground.

### How Often Should You Clean Your Gutters?

Most homes benefit from gutter cleaning **once or twice a year**, typically in the spring and fall. However, if your home is surrounded by trees that drop leaves or pine needles, you may need more frequent cleanings—every 3 to 4 months.
