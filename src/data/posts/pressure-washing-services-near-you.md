## Pressure Washing vs. Soft Washing: What’s the Difference?

**Pressure Washing**

High pressure

Best for concrete, brick, and hard surfaces

**Soft Washing**

Low pressure + cleaning solutions

Best for siding, roofs, and delicate materials

Knowing when to use each method is critical — and one of the biggest reasons to hire a professional instead of DIY.
